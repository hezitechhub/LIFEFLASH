Have a Question?

  • Why should I choose LIFEFLASH?

    →

    There is a shortage of Doctors and Nurses across Zambia, and many patients are unable to see a Medical Practitioners for weeks. Our aim is to provide high-quality healthcare in a timely manner so you can start feeling better today! Our Practitioners are Specialist Doctors and Nurses which means they are Registered with the Health Professiones Council of Zambia (HPCZ).

  • How do video or phone consultations work?

    →

    You will need a smartphone, tablet or laptop with a camera and a good internet connection. Follow the link we send you via email,WhatsApp or SMS.Click the link and join the virtual waiting room. The doctor will admit you and your consult begins.Your consultation will last up to 15 minutes.

  • How will I know if my medical issues can be treated this way?

    →

    When you book with LIFEFLASH our clinical team will contact you before your scheduled appointment and ask a few simple questions that will help us determine whether a phone or video consult is right for your needs. Our clinical team may ask you to send photos if needed to assist the Doctor with the consultation.

  • How do I pay?

    →

    You can pay using your Medical Insurance, Mobile money or Visa Card.

  • Is it secure and private?

    →

    Just like an in-person consult with your doctor or nurse, your Online Doctor consultation will be private and confidential. The systems used by Online LIFEFLASH Doctors meet recommended standards to protect the privacy and security of your visit. Your information is not stored in the online platform.

  • What happens if there are technical/connection problems?

    →

    If there are issues with your video connection e.g. the picture stops/freezes or the sound stops, the doctor will immediately phone you on your mobile number to assist or to complete the consultation on the phone.

  • How can I get my prescription?

    →

    Our trained clinical team will assess your needs and can send your prescription to your chosen local pharmacy.

  • Can I cancel or change my appointment?

    →

    All service users have a freedom to change or cancel the appointment.Under the conditions that there is refund for fuel used or expenses incured.
